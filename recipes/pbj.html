Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp fruit preserves
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps:

  1. Toast each slice of bread
  2. With a table knife, gently spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. With a different knife, gracefully spread the fruit preserves on the other slice of bread
  4. Place one slice of bread on top of the other so that the fillings are inside
  5. Cut the sandwich diagonally into two slices
