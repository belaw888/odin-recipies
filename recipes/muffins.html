Chocolate Chip Muffins

Description:

This is a delectable and easy recipe that is a chocolate lover's dream! This is of course a heirloom recipe passed down and perfected in my family for generations and not the first thing I found on Google.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup 2% milk
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. In a large bowl, combine the first 4 ingredients. In a small bowl, beat egg, milk and oil. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in chocolate chips.
  2. Fill 12 greased or paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean, 18-20 minutes. Cool for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack.
