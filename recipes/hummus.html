Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp tahini sauce

Steps:

  1. Combine chickpeas, cumin, garlic, and tahini sauce in a food processor
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy
  3. Pour into a bowl and drizzle with olive oil
